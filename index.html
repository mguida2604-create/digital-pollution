// features

Everything you need
to go digital-clean.

🖼️
Photo Swiper

Swipe right to delete, left to keep. Every photo you delete saves measurable CO₂. Rob8's hunger meter rises as your library gets lighter.

// Hunger tab
⏱️
Screen Time Shield

Using Apple's Family Controls, the app monitors selected apps and automatically blocks them after 2 hours — protecting Rob8's energy stat in real time.

// Energy tab
📚
Interactive Quizzes

4 data-backed quiz questions on streaming, email, data centers, and more. Every correct answer increases Rob8's education meter.

// Learn tab
🎧
Eco Podcasts

Listen to curated podcasts on sustainability, green tech, and digital footprints. Listening boosts Rob8's fun meter and your awareness.

// Fun tab
🤖
Rob8 Mood System

4 live stats — Hunger, Energy, Education, Fun — decay over time. Rob8's mood changes based on whichever stat is lowest. Keep them balanced.

// Home tab
🔔
Smart Notifications

Get gentle nudges from Rob8 when a stat drops below 50%. Different messages for each stat, with a cooldown to avoid spam.

// how it works

Simple by design.

01
Meet Rob8

A 5-slide onboarding introduces Rob8 and explains what data pollution is, why it matters, and how you can reduce it.

02
Grant Permissions

Allow Screen Time access via Family Controls and Photo Library access to unlock all features. Everything stays on your device.

03
Take Daily Actions

Delete photos, limit screen time, answer quizzes, listen to podcasts. Every action boosts one of Rob8's four stats.

04
Track Your Impact

See CO₂ saved per session and watch Rob8's mood improve as your digital habits change for the better.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data We Collect

Digital Pollution does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data (screen time, usage stats, game progress, photo interactions) is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

Family Controls & Screen Time

The app uses Apple's Family Controls framework to monitor device usage. This data is processed locally by Apple's system and is never accessible by us.

Photo Library

The app requests Photo Library access solely to display photos in the swipe-to-delete feature. No photos are uploaded or stored externally.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for families including minors. No personal data is collected from users of any age.
